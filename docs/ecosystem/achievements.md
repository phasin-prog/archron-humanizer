# Achievements

Achievements reflect **quality**, not quantity.

## Achievement Categories

### Milestone Achievements

| Achievement | Requirement | Reputation Bonus |
|-------------|-------------|------------------|
| First Words | First Article published | +10 |
| Knowledge Builder | 10 Articles published | +30 |
| Concept Creator | 10 Concepts created | +25 |
| Library Builder | 10 Books catalogued | +20 |
| Reference Master | 100 References added | +40 |
| Reviewer | 25 Reviews completed | +35 |
| Guide Maker | 5 Guides published | +50 |
| Cartographer | 20 Collections created | +20 |

## Achievement Rules

- Achievements are awarded automatically when criteria are met
- Achievement badges are displayed on the Profile
- Achievements can be featured on the Profile (max 6 visible)
- Reputation bonuses only apply once per achievement
- Achievements are never revoked (even if content is later archived)
- Special achievements may have manual validation by an Editor
- Achievement notifications are non-intrusive (silent notification)
