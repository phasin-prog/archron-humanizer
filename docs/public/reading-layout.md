## Reading Rules

- **Definition is always visible without scrolling** вҖ” no wall of text
- Related content is accessible from every page вҖ” not just at the bottom
- Sidebar is generated automatically from the Object's relationships
- Every section is collapsible on mobile
- No pagination вҖ” all content on one page
- Reading time badge is always visible
- "Continue where you left off" вҖ” future: reading progress saved
