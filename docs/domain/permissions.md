## Supporter Benefits

- Supporter badge on profile
- Direct dialogue with the creator (philosophy/design discussion)

## Writer Requirements

- Account age ≥ 7 days
- Completed at least 1 Guide
- Accepted Contributor Agreement

## Reviewer Requirements

- Published ≥ 5 Articles
- Domain expertise verified by Editor
- No active content violations

## Editor Appointment

- Nominated by existing Editor
- Approved by Administrator
- Demonstrates consistent quality reviewing
