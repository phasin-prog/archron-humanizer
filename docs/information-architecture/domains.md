# Knowledge Domains

Design the "knowledge structure", not the "page structure".

## Domains

1. **Psychology** — Mind, behavior, cognition, personality, development, psychopathology
2. **Philosophy** — Metaphysics, epistemology, ethics, logic, aesthetics, existentialism
3. **Anthropology** — Culture, society, ritual, kinship, evolution, human diversity
4. **History** — Events, eras, civilizations, historiography, intellectual history
5. **Language** — Linguistics, semantics, etymology, grammar, communication
6. **Mythology** — Myth, archetype, folklore, hero journey, sacred narrative
7. **Religion** — Theology, spirituality, practice, scripture, comparative religion
8. **Science** — Natural science, neuroscience, biology, physics, systems theory
9. **Symbolism** — Symbol, sign, allegory, iconography, semiotics
10. **Art** — Visual art, literature, music, architecture, aesthetic expression
11. **AI & Future** — Artificial intelligence, transhumanism, digital mind, future studies
12. **Civilization** — Society, governance, economy, law, institutions, progress
