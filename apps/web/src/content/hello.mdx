# Welcome to ARCHRON

## A Human Knowledge Platform

ARCHRON organizes, connects, and transforms multidisciplinary knowledge about human beings
into an interconnected knowledge ecosystem.

### What We Catalog

- **Concepts** — Core psychological and philosophical ideas
- **Thinkers** — Philosophers, psychologists, and intellectuals
- **Schools** — Intellectual traditions
- **Books** — Published reference works
- **Articles** — Long-form analytical essays

> Knowledge is a living network, not isolated pages.
